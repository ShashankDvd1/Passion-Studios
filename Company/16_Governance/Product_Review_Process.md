# Product Review Process
## 1. Vision
Ensure every feature aligns with player needs, business goals, and the company vision before development starts.

## 6. Requirements
### 6.1 The Seven-Question Gate
All product reviews center on the Seven-Question Gate (`02-002`). A feature that fails the gate is rejected or sent for revision.

### 6.2 Founder Sign-Off
- All P0 and P1 features (core mechanics, monetization, major systems) require explicit Founder approval via the Founder Agent's decision queue.
- P2 and P3 features are reviewed by the Product Manager Agent against the established strategic roadmap.
