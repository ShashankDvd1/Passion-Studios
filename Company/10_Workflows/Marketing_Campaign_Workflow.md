# Marketing Campaign Workflow
## Trigger
Manual — Marketing Agent or Founder identifies a campaign need (launch, event, seasonal).
## Steps
1. **Campaign Brief** (Marketing Agent) → Goals, audience, channels, budget, timeline
2. **Creative Brief** (Content Creator Agent) → Copy, messaging, tone alignment with Studio Identity
3. **Asset Preparation** (Art Director Agent) → Visual assets matching brand guidelines
4. **Store Listing Update** (ASO Agent) → Keywords, screenshots, description updates if needed
5. **Campaign Launch** (Marketing Agent) → Deploy across channels
6. **Performance Tracking** (Analytics Agent) → CPI, ROAS, organic lift monitoring
7. **Campaign Post-Mortem** (Marketing Agent + Analytics Agent) → Results vs. goals, learnings
## Governance: P1 founder approval on budget >$500. P2 for within approved budget.
## Estimated Duration: 5-14 days
