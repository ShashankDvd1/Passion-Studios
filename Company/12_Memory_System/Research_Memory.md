# Research Memory
## Mission
Store external knowledge: market data, competitive intelligence, technology scouting, behavioral research, and industry trends. The "outside world" memory layer.
## Content Categories
| Category | What's Stored | Expiry |
|----------|-------------|--------|
| **Competitive Intelligence** | Competitor features, updates, strategies, reviews | 6 months rolling |
| **Market Data** | Industry trends, revenue benchmarks, audience data | 12 months rolling |
| **Technology Scouting** | Emerging tech evaluations (AI, AR, platforms) | 12 months rolling |
| **Behavioral Research** | Psychology papers, engagement studies | Permanent |
| **Industry Reports** | Market reports, analyst predictions | 12 months rolling |
| **Player Benchmarks** | Genre-level retention/monetization benchmarks | Updated quarterly |
## Injected when: Product, Strategy, or Innovation agents need external context.
