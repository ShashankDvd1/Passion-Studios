# Design Token Library

This is a reusable company asset for standardizing emotional and mechanical interactions across all Studio OS games. It ensures that every time a player achieves something, the sensory feedback is consistent, scalable, and emotionally resonant.

| Token ID | Name | Category | Purpose | Player Emotion | Animation | Audio | VFX | Haptic Feedback | UI Feedback | Trigger Conditions | Cooldown Rules | Unity Mapping | Accessibility Notes | Performance Notes | Reusability Guidelines |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| TKN-001 | Item Discovered | Discovery Token | Acknowledge a first-time find. | Curiosity / Delight | Slight pop-in scale punch (1.2x -> 1.0x). | High-pitched chime (`sfx_chime_01`). | Sparkle burst around the object. | Light tick. | "New!" toast notification at top of screen. | `DiscoveryManager.HasDiscovered()` returns true. | 1 second global cooldown to prevent spam. | `VFX_Sparkle_Burst`, `UI_Toast_Controller` | Ensure toast is screen-reader friendly and high contrast. | Use object pooling for the sparkle VFX. | Use for any item, crop, fish, or mechanic unlocked for the first time. |
| TKN-002 | Micro-Reward | Reward Token | Acknowledge minor progress (e.g., chopping wood). | Competence | Resource icon flies to inventory icon. | Satisfying thud/click (`sfx_wood_chop`). | Small dust poof at source. | None. | Resource counter flashes white. | Action completed successfully. | None (can be spammed). | `UI_FlyToInventory_Anim`, `VFX_Dust_Poof` | Ensure color blind safe resource icons. | Do not use heavy particles; 3-5 sprites max. | Standardize flight speed and easing curves for all resources. |
| TKN-003 | Major Milestone | Celebration Token | Celebrate a level-up or house upgrade. | Pride | Time freeze (0.1s), then confetti explosion. | Orchestral fanfare (`sfx_fanfare_01`). | Screen-space confetti overlay. | Heavy double vibration. | Full screen modal with stat increases. | `ProgressionManager.LevelUp` fired. | N/A | `VFX_Confetti_ScreenSpace`, `UI_Milestone_Modal` | Allow skipping the animation for photosensitive users. | Pre-load fanfare audio to prevent stutter on trigger. | Only use for major emotional highs; do not dilute the impact. |
