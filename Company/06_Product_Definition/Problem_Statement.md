# Problem Statement: Tiny World

## Executive Summary
**Wonder Forge Studios** requires a foundational, commercially viable mobile game to establish its AI-native development pipeline and build the underlying "Wonder Forge Game OS." The goal is to rapidly ship an MVP within a strict 30-day timeline that prioritizes emotional player connection over predatory monetization tactics.

## The Core Problem
Modern mobile games rely heavily on aggressive engagement tactics, endless tapping, and manufactured friction. Players often experience burnout rather than genuine enjoyment or emotional investment. 

**Tiny World** aims to solve this by creating a mobile experience designed around **emotional attachment**. The game must encourage players to return naturally by fostering feelings of:
- Ownership
- Curiosity
- Meaningful Progress
- Positive Habit Formation
- Personal Investment

Furthermore, from a studio perspective, the problem is **how to build a game quickly while ensuring the technology is reusable** for future titles without over-engineering the initial product.

## The Solution: Tiny World MVP
Tiny World will be a mobile (Android) simulation/collection game. To meet the 30-day launch constraint and avoid speculative engineering, the MVP scope is strictly limited to the essential systems required to prove the emotional core and establish the foundational Game OS architecture.

### Key MVP Features
1. **The World & Companion**: A single playable world featuring one interactive companion to anchor the emotional connection.
2. **Core Loop**: Resource collection, inventory management, and a basic economy to provide meaningful progression.
3. **Player Expression**: Simple building placement to foster ownership.
4. **Retention Mechanics**: Daily rewards, missions, and offline progression to create positive habit loops.
5. **Infrastructure**: Save systems, analytics, and rewarded ads to support the business model and gather real-world player feedback.

## Business & Technical Constraints
- **Platform:** Android (Google Play).
- **Timeline:** 30-day MVP launch.
- **Development Philosophy:** AI-assisted Vibe Coding (80% Building / 20% Planning).
- **Engineering Rules:** Mobile-optimized, zero-allocation where possible, event-driven, and highly decoupled. The architecture must extract reusable systems into the "Game OS" naturally as they are validated by gameplay, rather than planning a massive engine upfront.

## Definition of Success
The Tiny World MVP will be considered successful when it:
1. Ships to the Google Play Store within the 30-day window.
2. Successfully establishes the Wonder Forge Game OS infrastructure (e.g., Event Bus, Runtime Core, Save Systems).
3. Gathers real player analytics and feedback to guide Phase 2 development.
4. Proves that an AI-assisted Solo Founder workflow can yield a maintainable, high-quality commercial product.
